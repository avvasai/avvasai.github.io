Idea
Why did you pick this idea to work on? Do you have domain expertise in this area? How do you know people need what you're making?
"We live in loops as tight and as closed as the hosts do, seldom questioning our choices, content, for the most part, to be told what to do next." We loved watching Westworld and want to make robots in houses and industry common. More specifically we want to build a dynamic catalogue of skills that robots can pull and do test time training on during deployment. We have experience building Data Acquisition systems and want to leverage that to build custom data acquisition hardware to create worthwhile datasets. Robotics researchers at leading labs we have spoken with, are enthusiastic about getting such data
Who are your competitors? What do you understand about your business that they don't?
Tons of competitors Larger Companies (Micro1, Scale, Surge) Smaller Companies (MicroAGI, BuildAI) YC Companies (HumanArchive, Sensei, Cortex, Spatial) Interaction that competition does not capture or annotate well - Learning new skills - Being instructed on new tasks - Exception cases (Failing at a task) - Working with various instruction modalities (PDF for Ikea, Assembly videos for Legos) Modalities that competition does not capture - Gripping Force - Temperature (How do you know if something is hot/cold enough) - Chain of thought audio for self annotation - Full body pose - We focus on capturing how humans instruct and teach other humans as learning that skill is important for robots to be deployed successfully - We DO NOT want to capture in-the-wild egocentric data as it is only useful for pre training and highly commoditized - We know how to make our OWN economical hardware to match the input modalities for target robots The juicier market is in post training data, NOT pre training data
How do or will you make money? How much could you make?
Revenue from licensing datasets of skills we capture Foundation model companies are spending billions on high quality human data We expect to make similar if not larger amounts as physical ai does not have an existing internet scale dataset
